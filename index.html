What It Is

Jul 11th, 2013

Hey

You seem to have stumbled upon this website, or “blog”. Let me assure you, this blog is not likely to be remotely useful to you. This blog is almost entirely created to assist me in remembering how to use a computer in certain ways. If, however, you also have an interest in computing that intersects with my own, there may be articles within this blog that will assist you.

Types of Projects

The main types of projects on this blog will be entirely software based and will either be derived from my experiences as a computer science student, or from extra-curricular projects I have done on my own. Perhaps unsurprisingly (since they are my own) I find all of these projects incredibly interesting, even when they aren’t even close to being interesting. In case this is relevant, the main languages in which I develop are:

C/C++ (I hate C++) Python Ruby Perl Javascript Shell Scripting

and probably others, but these are definitely a relatively thorough selection of those with which I am most familiar.
